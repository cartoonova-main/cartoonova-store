Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Thank you for shopping at Simpson Me! This Shipping Policy explains how we deliver your custom Simpsons-style portraits.

1. Digital Delivery

All orders include digital delivery of your custom portrait.

Delivery Timeline

  • First Draft: 2-3 business days after order placement
  • Revisions: 1-2 business days per revision request
  • Final Files: Delivered within 24 hours of your approval

File Formats

Your digital portrait will be delivered via email in high-resolution formats suitable for:

  • Printing at home or professional print shops
  • Sharing on social media
  • Using as digital wallpapers

2. Physical Products (Printed Merchandise)

If you ordered printed products (canvas, poster, framed print, etc.), here's what to expect:

Production Time

After you approve your final portrait design:

  • Production: 2-5 business days
  • Quality check: Each item is inspected before shipping

Shipping Times

Destination Estimated Delivery
United States 4-8 business days
Canada 6-10 business days
Europe 7-12 business days
Australia/New Zealand 8-14 business days
Rest of World 10-20 business days

Note: These are estimates. Actual delivery times may vary due to customs processing, holidays, or carrier delays.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on:

  • Your location
  • Product size and weight
  • Shipping method selected

Free Shipping: We offer free worldwide shipping on select products and orders over a certain amount. Check our current promotions at checkout.

4. Order Tracking

Once your physical order ships:

  • You'll receive a shipping confirmation email
  • A tracking number will be provided
  • Track your package through the carrier's website

5. International Orders

Customs and Import Duties

For international orders:

  • Customs fees, import duties, and taxes may apply
  • These charges are the customer's responsibility
  • We cannot predict or control these fees as they vary by country
  • Contact your local customs office for more information

Delivery Delays

International shipments may experience delays due to:

  • Customs clearance procedures
  • Local postal service processing
  • Remote delivery locations

6. Shipping Address

Please ensure your shipping address is correct and complete. We are not responsible for:

  • Packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer
  • Packages that cannot be delivered due to incomplete address information

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, contact us immediately at support@simpsonme.com.

7. Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged:

  • Contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date
  • We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue
  • Replacements or refunds will be provided as per our Refund Policy

8. P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO Addresses

We can ship to P.O. Boxes and military APO/FPO addresses. Please note that delivery times may be longer for these addresses.
